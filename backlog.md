# Product Backlog: Blog and Home Interface

This backlog outlines the tasks and user stories for the **Blog** and **Home Interface** modules.

## 🏠 Home Interface

| ID | User Story / Task | Priority | Status | Estimation (Story Points) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| HI-01 | **Hero Section:** As a user, I want to see a welcoming hero section introducing the EcoSpot platform. | High | To Do | 3 |
| HI-02 | **Navigation Bar:** As a user, I want a responsive top navigation bar to access Home, Blog, Events, and Login. | High | To Do | 2 |
| HI-03 | **Featured Content:** As a user, I want to see recent blog posts and upcoming events highlighted on the home page. | Medium | To Do | 5 |
| HI-04 | **Footer:** As a user, I want to see a footer containing quick links, social media icons, and contact information. | Low | To Do | 1 |
| HI-05 | **Responsive Design:** Ensure the home interface adapts seamlessly to desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. | High | To Do | 3 |

## 📝 Blog Module

| ID | User Story / Task | Priority | Status | Estimation (Story Points) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| BL-01 | **View Blog Posts:** As a user, I want to view a paginated list of all published blog posts. | High | To Do | 3 |
| BL-02 | **Read Article:** As a user, I want to click on a blog card to read the full article details. | High | To Do | 2 |
| BL-03 | **Create Post (CRUD):** As an author, I want a form to create new blog posts with a title, content, image, and tags. | High | To Do | 5 |
| BL-04 | **Edit/Delete Post (CRUD):** As an author, I want to be able to edit or delete my existing blog posts. | High | To Do | 3 |
| BL-05 | **Tag Filtering:** As a user, I want to filter the blog posts by specific tags/categories to find relevant content. | Medium | To Do | 3 |
| BL-06 | **Search Bar:** As a user, I want to search for blog posts by keywords or title. | Medium | To Do | 2 |
| BL-07 | **Comments Section:** As a user, I want to leave comments on a blog post and view comments from others. | Low | To Do | 4 |
